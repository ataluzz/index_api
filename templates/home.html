With this API you can see statistics for your directory or file, such as:

  • List of contained directories (only for directories)
  • List of contained files (only for directories)
  • Number of files (only for directories)

And statistics for files with .txt, .docx, .md or .py extension (or you can choose files with specific extension(s) in form below):

  • 5 most frequent words
  • 5 of most rare words
  • Medium length of word
  • Percentage of vowels
  • Percentage of consonants
  • Number of syllables

Also you can get statistics for specific word, such as:

  • How many times word is found in directory/file
  • Number of vowels
  • Number of consonants
  • Number of syllables
